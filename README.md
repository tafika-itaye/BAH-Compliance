# BAH Compliance & Quality Platform

Policies, SOPs, KPIs, Near Misses & Incidents management for **Blantyre Adventist Hospital**.

## What's Real

- 8 seed policies/SOPs with attestation tracking (percentages, staff counts)
- 6 seed incidents across severity levels (no-harm, near-miss, minor, major, sentinel)
- 8 KPIs with target vs actual, trend indicators, and met/at-risk status
- 5 corrective actions linked to incidents
- Incident reporting with anonymous option
- Incident lifecycle: Open → Investigating → Closed with investigation timeline
- COHSASA accreditation readiness gauge and 10-point checklist
- Policy detail views with version control metadata
- Attestation simulation (click to attest)
- All data persisted in localStorage

## Simulated

- Email/SMS notifications on incident reports
- PDF export of policies
- Automated review date reminders
- Multi-user concurrent attestation
- Integration with hospital HIS
